The Texts & Words area is where you work with your text corpus and the words in texts.

With the wordforms (words) in texts, you can

Here are Help topics that help you learn the various things you can do with the words in texts and the various ways to accomplish them:

Interlinear Texts overview lists all the tabs.

Parsing words overview gives an important overview for using the Analyze tab.

Analyze text overview lists tasks that you typically do manually in the Analyze tab.

Specify a word gloss gives steps to manually only add word glosses.

Concordance overview and Complex Concordance help you with basic or more complex searching of texts.

Word List Concordance:




Interlinear Texts, Concordance and Word List Concordance each display the same set of tabs (Info, Baseline, Gloss, Analyze, Tagging and Text Chart). You can do the same work in each tool, but they provide different ways to find or concord on words, forms, morphemes, analyses and so on.

Word Analyses allows you to approve and assign analyses, change spelling and to specify the spelling status of vernacular words.

Bulk Edit Wordforms allows you to change multiple words (or metadata) at the same time, including spelling status.
